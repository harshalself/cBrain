## 1. Introduction

Company Brain is an AI-powered internal knowledge and productivity platform designed for startups and growing companies. It transforms scattered company documents, policies, and technical information into a single intelligent system that answers employee questions, trains new hires, and gives management deep insights into organizational knowledge health.

Instead of employees searching through PDFs, Notion pages, Slack messages, and Google Docs, they simply ask Company Brain and get instant, accurate, and contextual answers.

---

## 2. The Problem We Are Solving

Modern companies face several serious internal challenges:

* Important information is scattered across multiple tools (Notion, Google Drive, Slack, GitHub, internal wikis)
* Employees repeatedly ask the same questions to HR and tech leads
* Documentation becomes outdated quickly
* New hires take months to become productive
* Managers have no visibility into what employees are confused about

This leads to:

* Wasted time
* Slower onboarding
* Lower productivity
* Increased load on HR and senior engineers

## 5. Core Features

### 5.1 AI Knowledge Assistant

Employees can ask questions such as:

* "How do I apply for leave?"
* "What is our deployment process?"
* "How does authentication work?"
* "Where is the API documentation?"

The AI responds using company-approved documents only and provides the exact source reference.

---

### 5.2 Smart Knowledge Base Manager

* Upload PDFs, DOCX, Markdown, TXT
* Sync with Notion, GitHub, Google Drive
* Auto versioning of documents
* Metadata tagging (team, department, date)

---

### 5.3 AI Onboarding Coach

When a new employee joins, Company Brain creates a personalized learning journey:

* Day-wise onboarding plan
* Micro-learning lessons
* Interactive Q&A
* Quizzes and checkpoints

This reduces onboarding time drastically.

---

### 5.4 Knowledge Health Monitoring

The system continuously analyzes:

* Most asked questions
* Frequently flagged answers
* Outdated documents
* Missing documentation topics

Admins receive actionable insights like:

* "Leave policy document is outdated"
* "Deployment process documentation is missing"
* "Most confusion is about authentication flow"

---

### 5.5 Analytics Dashboard

Admins and managers can view:

* Daily active users
* Popular topics
* Knowledge gap heatmap
* Onboarding progress
* Department-wise confusion trends
